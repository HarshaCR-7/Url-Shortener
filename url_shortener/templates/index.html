What is a RSS Feed Mixer?

  • An RSS feed is a file that contains a summary of updates from a website, often in the form of a list of articles with links.
  • RSS is used by people to stay informed about news, podcasts, and other information online.
  • RSS Feed Mixer can be used to mix multiple RSS Feeds and get feeds on topics of your interest.

How to use RSS Feed Mixer?

  • Enter the URL address of each feed.
  • Press Add button to add multiple feeds.
  • Enter feed per url to limit feeds per url.
  • Press Submit to create your personal RSS feed.
